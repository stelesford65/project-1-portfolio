My love for creativity
❮ ❯

I’m a passionate and ambitious software engineer ready to take on a new opportunity to problem solve and help to make the lives of others easier.I foster thispassionbecause I am very creative and love to bring my ideas and/or the ideas of others to life through complex logic and design. I dream creativity and can produce visually stunning and fast programs. I’m also a problem solver. As a problem solver, I love to break problems down into simple steps and solve each one until the problem is completely eradicated. My various work experiences throughout my life has taught me to be a leader, to use critical thinking, to never give up, to learn and discover, and to share my knowledge with others. I’ve been in situations in which I thought I wouldn’t succeed but my natural urge to power through the worst of situations always brought me to success and efficiency. I love to see how much of a positive effect software has on life as we know it and would love to take my software engineering career to the next level as a team leader, team player, or even individual.
